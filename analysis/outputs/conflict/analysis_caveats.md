# Conflict-measles exploratory analysis caveats

- These are association models only; they do not identify causal effects.
- Conflict variables come from UCDP GED event locations (1989-2024); years outside this range have no conflict coverage.
- Measles cases are reported cases and may suffer from under-reporting, especially during conflict.
- Country-level annual aggregation masks subnational and within-year dynamics.
- No country fixed effects are included in these starter models; add FE/DiD/specification checks in follow-up.
